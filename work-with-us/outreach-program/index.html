Draconis Robotics Outreach

Free Robotics Workshops for Children by Request

Empowering Young Minds Through Robotics

We believe that every child deserves access to technology education. Our outreach program offers free robotics workshops to charities, NGOs, shelters, and community centers.

What We Offer

  • Hands-on robotics building & coding sessions
  • Beginner-friendly, no prior experience needed
  • All equipment and materials provided
  • Workshops tailored for ages 7–16
  • Conducted at your location or our academy

Who Can Request

  • Registered charities or community-based NGOs
  • Children's homes, shelters, or youth organizations
  • Schools in low-income or underserved areas
  • Initiatives with a focus on education or youth development
