Welcome Our First Draconis Talents Creator

Introducing Guavxx — The perfect blend of gaming passion and robotics expertise

Known for sharp humor, interactive streams, and a competitive edge, Guavxx has made his mark as an ex-3-time Platinum Apex Legends player and ex-VRC (VEX Robotics Competition) competitor. Now, he’s taking his talents to the next level by becoming the first official content creator signed to Draconis Talents.

“When Mythiacosss reached out to me and presented this project, I was genuinely amazed by what he’s building. The vision behind Draconis Robotics and what it stands for really impressed me—it’s about more than just competition; it’s about community, growth, and creating something new. I’m beyond excited to be part of that.”
— Guavxx
“I’ve been watching Guavxx’s streams for quite a while now, and I’m genuinely surprised by what he brings to the table—both from a content perspective and a competitive one. His passion for VEX and his knowledge of the space really stood out to me. We’re thrilled to welcome him to Draconis and can’t wait to see what he’ll do next.”
— Mythiacosss, Founder of Draconis Robotics

At Draconis, we’re bringing together creators, competitors, and innovators who want to make an impact. Guavxx is the first of many—but his unique background in both tech and gaming makes him the perfect person to lead this new wave.

Welcome to the Draconis family, Guavxx. 🐉🎮

Follow Guavxx on Twitter @Guavxx and catch his streams on Twitch twitch.tv/guavxx.
