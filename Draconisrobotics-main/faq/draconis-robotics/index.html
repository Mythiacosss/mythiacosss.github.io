Frequently Asked Questions

Draconis Robotics is a specialized robotics education academy focused on teaching coding and STEM skills through hands-on VEX Robotics programs and competitions.

Our VEX Robotics classes are open to kids, teens, and beginners with no prior experience, as well as advanced learners interested in robotics and coding.

No experience is necessary! We start from the basics of VEX Robotics and build up skills progressively.

Classes are held in-person at our main academy facility equipped with dedicated robotics and streaming rooms.

Our pricing depends on the specific VEX Robotics course and its duration. For detailed information and the latest rates, please contact our team directly. We’re happy to help you find the best option for your needs.

We are committed to accessibility and occasionally provide scholarships or sponsorships. Contact us for details.

Yes! We partner with schools and NGOs to deliver free or sponsored VEX Robotics workshops and outreach programs.

We exclusively use the VEX Robotics platform, which is recognized globally for education and competitions.

All of our VEX Robotics classes are conducted in-person at our academy to provide hands-on learning with real equipment and direct instructor support. We do not offer online classes at this time.

Enrollment can be completed through our website or by contacting our team directly.

To maintain quality instruction, class sizes are capped at 20 students.

We maintain strict safety protocols during classes, including supervised equipment use and adherence to health guidelines.

To ensure a focused learning environment, parents are highly discouraged from attending classes. We provide regular progress updates and parent meetings instead.
