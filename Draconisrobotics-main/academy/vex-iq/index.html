VEX IQ Academy

VEX IQ is an engaging, easy-to-learn robotics platform designed for students aged 7 to 12, introducing foundational STEM skills through hands-on building and coding.

Our academy guides learners from beginner to mastery through structured curriculum levels.

Curriculum Levels

Structured learning path to build skills progressively:

  • Level 1 - Foundations: Basic robotics concepts, simple builds, and introductory programming logic.
  • Level 2 - Intermediate: Sensor integration, advanced builds, and autonomous programming.
  • Level 3 - Advanced: Complex robot designs, strategic real worlds problem-solving, and advanced programming concepts.
  • Level 4 - Mastery: Competition preparation, custom designs, and teamwork skills.
