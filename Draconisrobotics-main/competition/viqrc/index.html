VEX IQ Robotics Competition (VIQRC)

The official VEX IQ Competition for elementary and middle school students.

About the VEX IQ Robotics Competition

The VEX IQ Robotics Competition (VIQRC) introduces younger students to the world of competitive robotics. Students build robots using plastic snap-together parts and program them to complete engaging game challenges. At Draconis Robotics, we guide beginners and intermediate students from zero to hero with structured mentorship, workshops, and scrimmages.

Hands-On Building

Design, build, and improve robots using VEX IQ modular parts and sensors.

Introductory Coding

Learn block-based and Python programming using VEXcode IQ.

Teamwork & Strategy

Work in alliances, develop teamwork, and compete in both driver and autonomous matches.

Current Season's Game

Watch the official game reveal for this season's VEX IQ Robotics Competition challenge.

Participant Requirements

To participate in VIQRC with Draconis Robotics, students must meet the following criteria:

  • Be in elementary or middle school (ages 8-14)
  • Attend at least 4 training sessions before joining competitions
  • Participate in a readiness review and or interview with our coaches
