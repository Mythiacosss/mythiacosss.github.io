VEX V5 Robotics Competition (V5RC)

The official VEX Robotics Competition for middle school and high school students.

About the VEX V5 Robotics Competition

The VEX V5 Robotics Competition (V5RC) is the world's largest and most prestigious robotics competition for middle and high school students. Students design, build, and program robots to compete in dynamic games that change every season. At Draconis Robotics, we provide continuous training, scrimmages, and mentorship to prepare our students for success.

Engineering Skills

Hands-on building with advanced mechanical design, electronics, and CAD modeling.

Programming & Automation

Learn to program autonomous and driver-controlled systems using VEXcode.

Strategic Gameplay

Develop strategies, work in alliances, and compete in team-based challenges.

Current Season's Game

Watch the official game reveal for this season's VEX V5RC challenge.

Participant Requirements

To participate in V5RC with Draconis Robotics, students must meet the following criteria:

  • Be in middle school or high school (ages 11-18)
  • Have prior robotics experience OR be enrolled in our classes for at least 1 month
  • Pass a technical assessment test
  • Complete an interview with our coaching team
