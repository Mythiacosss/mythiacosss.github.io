Company Overview

Draconis Robotics (DRGN) is a specialist robotics academy and team focused exclusively on VEX Robotics Competition (VRC). Founded by former VRC competitors and robotics enthusiasts, DRGN combines competitive experience with dedicated training to help students and teams excel in VEX Robotics.

Our Vision

To be the premier VEX Robotics training academy that nurtures competitive excellence and inspires innovation through hands-on learning — accessible to all.

Our Mission

  • Provide expert coaching and training rooted in firsthand VEX Robotics Competition experience.
  • Develop well-rounded competitors by emphasizing strategy, engineering design, coding, and teamwork.
  • Foster a supportive community of passionate robotics students aiming for competitive success.
  • Empower students to reach their full potential through structured learning and real-world robotics challenges.
  • Keep training affordable — because STEM skills will be essential for 75% of future jobs, and no child should be denied the joy of learning due to cost.
  • Offer sponsorships and financial aid for students who cannot afford fees, ensuring equal opportunities for all.
  • Conduct outreach sessions to introduce robotics and STEM education to underrepresented and underserved communities.

Key Offerings

  • VEX Robotics Training & Coaching: Comprehensive courses and mentorship from former VRC players, covering robot design, programming, and competition strategy.
  • Competitive Team Development: Form and support competitive VEX teams for local, regional, and international competitions.
  • Workshops & Outreach: Regular clinics, bootcamps, and outreach sessions designed to sharpen skills, raise STEM awareness, and prepare teams for competition season.

Our Edge

With deep roots in the VRC community and hands-on competition experience, DRGN brings insider knowledge and proven techniques that help teams perform at their best — while ensuring training remains the most affordable in the market.

Facilities

Currently operating from a 1100 sqft dedicated space, our facility includes training rooms, a streaming studio, and areas equipped for robot building and testing — designed to support a focused competitive environment. We plan to expand soon to better serve our growing community.

A Message from the CEO

In this day and age, many kids grow up sitting in classrooms, listening to teachers talk — but often they miss out on practical skills that truly prepare them for the future. For many, studying feels like a chore because the joy and fun have been stripped away from learning. Sadly, creativity is being stripped away too, as rigid curriculums leave little room for imagination and hands-on exploration.

At Draconis Robotics, we believe education should be hands-on, engaging, and inspiring. Beyond teaching STEM skills, which are essential for tomorrow’s jobs, we are committed to building perseverance, resilience, and character. Robotics isn’t just about robots — it’s about developing problem solvers who don’t give up when faced with challenges, and creators who dare to imagine new possibilities.

We want to nurture more than just engineers; we want to help shape confident, creative individuals ready to take on the world.

— Mythiacosss, CEO, Draconis Robotics
