Our Story

Why we started.

Draconis Robotics began with a simple idea: turn passion into purpose. As former VEX Robotics competitors and lifelong fans of VTubers, we’ve experienced firsthand how technology, creativity, and community can change lives.

Our mission started with robotics—providing affordable, accessible STEM education to help anyone, regardless of background, learn, build, and innovate.

But as we grew, so did our vision. Inspired by the vibrant VTuber and digital creator communities we love, Draconis Robotics expanded to support not just engineers and roboticists, but creators of all kinds.

Today, we are a home for innovators—where robotics, STEM, and digital creativity collide. Whether you're building the next competition robot or crafting your online persona, we’re here to help you succeed.

Join us. Let’s build the future—together.
