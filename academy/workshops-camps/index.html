Holiday Camps & School Programs

Fun, hands-on robotics and coding experiences for students, schools, and community groups.

For Students & Parents: Holiday Camps

Join our holiday camps for an unforgettable robotics experience! Learn to build, code, and compete with VEX IQ & VEX V5 in a fun and supportive environment.
*We also offer flexible one-day camp options for schools and groups who prefer a shorter program.

VEX IQ Camp

Perfect for beginners aged 7–12. Learn robotics basics and team collaboration.

1-day & 3-day options

VEX V5 Camp

For students aged 13–16. Dive into advanced building, coding, and game strategy.

1-day & 3-day options

Game Challengers
