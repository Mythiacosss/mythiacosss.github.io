Sponsorship Program

At Draconis Robotics, we believe every child should have the opportunity to learn and grow in STEM, regardless of financial constraints. Our Sponsorship Program offers up to 75% off course fees to help talented and passionate students access our robotics and coding programs.

How to Qualify for Sponsorship

1. Apply Directly

Families who require financial assistance can approach us by submitting an application. We carefully review each case to understand your needs and determine how best to support you. Our goal is to make learning accessible without compromising quality.

2. Get Scouted

We actively scout for promising talent at our outreach programs and events. If you attend and demonstrate exceptional skills or enthusiasm, our team may reach out to discuss sponsorship options tailored to help you join our academy.

Why Sponsorship Matters

We want to nurture the next generation of innovators, engineers, and creators by removing financial barriers. Through sponsorship, we invest in your potential and provide the resources you need to succeed.
